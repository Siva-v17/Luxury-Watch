Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a full service take?
+

A complete disassembly, clean and re-regulation typically takes 6 to 10 weeks. Complex complications or restoration commissions may require 3 to 6 months. You'll receive a detailed timeline at consultation.

Do you service watches from other maisons?
+

Our master watchmakers are trained across Swiss and German movement families. We service selected third-party brands — contact us with the reference and we will confirm eligibility.

How does the bespoke commission process work?
+

We begin with a confidential brief — your aesthetic preferences, intended complications and personal narrative. Our atelier then prepares a concept dossier before any work begins. Full commissions take 18 to 36 months.

Is my piece insured during service?
+

Yes. Every piece in our care is covered by our own comprehensive fine-art and horological policy from the moment it leaves your hands to its return delivery.

Can I upgrade my membership tier?
+

Absolutely. Tier upgrades are prorated and take effect immediately. Your advisor can arrange this at any time — no forms, no waiting periods.
