Common Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit the boutique?
+

Walk-ins are always welcome during open hours, but private consultations and bespoke commissions are by appointment to ensure an advisor's full attention.

How quickly will an advisor respond?
+

All enquiries submitted through this form receive a response within 24 hours, Monday through Saturday.

Can I request a virtual consultation?
+

Yes. Select "General Enquiry" in the form and mention your preferred time zone — we'll arrange a video call with a specialist.

Do you ship internationally?
+

Every acquisition is hand-delivered or shipped fully insured to over 60 countries, with full customs documentation handled on your behalf.
